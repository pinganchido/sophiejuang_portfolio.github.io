Candies

Initially, the candy factory will generate ten candies with random shapes, colors, sizes, and patterns. Users can score candies by the scoreboard in the controller, and candies with higher scores will make new candies more likely with their feature.

Users can collect candies they like in the glass jar by dragging candies from the conveyor to the glass jar. And users can see the candies they have collected by clicking the glass jar.
Method of candies generating: Genetic Algorithm

Procedural Game Level Design

This project explores how procedural generation can be used to create 2D side-scrolling game levels. It focuses on two main techniques: graph grammar, which helps generate structured and connected level layouts, and the method used in Spelunky, known for its randomized and replayable level design. By combining these techniques, the project aims to develop a system that can automatically generate, varied, and playable game levels.
