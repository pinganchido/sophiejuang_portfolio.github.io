Light Up Lanterns


1. Light balls keep generating and moving. When a lightball is close to a lantern and a lantern has not attracted too much lightballs, it will be attracted by the lantern and change its color to the same as the lantern, and the lantern will be lighted up by a particle.
2. If a lantern has attracted more than three light balls, all of the light balls attracted by it will have the chance(10%) to leave a lantern. To prevent staying same situation for a long time.
3. If a lantern is not lighted up by light balls and a special lantern is moving away, a lantern will be lighted up automatically. To ensure every lantern can be lighted up after passing through by special lantern.
